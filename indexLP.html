About Upmesh

The Upmesh bookstore was created by four university students in a joint program between Carleton University and Algonquin College. While we started as school project we have now grown into a fully functionable store.

Order just about any book you can think of from Upmesh. We offer a wide variety of genres, age-groups and even languages. We frequently have sales and are always quick to restock your favoutite books when they become unavaliable. Upmesh is your go to place for book shopping!
